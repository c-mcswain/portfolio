How I Think

Technical Account Management is not just account coverage. It is understanding how a technical issue affects the customer, the internal team, and the business relationship at the same time.

Trust is built through ownership, clarity, and follow-through.

When technical complexity reaches the customer, the work is no longer just troubleshooting. It becomes coordination, translation, expectation-setting, and steady ownership across everyone involved.

I connect customer impact, internal context, and next actions so executives know what is happening, administrators feel supported, and Product or Engineering has the detail needed to move the issue forward.

My goal is to reduce friction before it compounds and make the customer experience feel actively owned, not passed around.
